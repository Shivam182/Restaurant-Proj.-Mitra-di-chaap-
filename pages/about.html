About us

Welcome to Mitra di chaap, where passion for food, exceptional hospitality, and a commitment to creating memorable dining experiences come together in harmony. Our journey began 1956 when a group of dedicated food enthusiasts and culinary experts came together to bring a unique and delectable dining experience to Haridwar/Uttrakhand.

Our Philosophy: At MDC, our philosophy is simple yet profound: to offer an inviting sanctuary where guests can escape the ordinary and embark on a culinary adventure that engages the senses. We believe in the power of food to create connections, forge memories, and celebrate life's moments, big and small.

Culinary Excellence: Our kitchen is the heart of our restaurant, where artistry and innovation meet tradition. Led by our talented chefs, we are dedicated to sourcing the finest, freshest, and locally-sourced ingredients to craft dishes that not only satisfy your palate but also tell a story of flavors and textures.

Diverse Menu: We take pride in offering a menu that caters to a variety of tastes and preferences. Whether you're a fan of classic comfort food or seek adventurous culinary explorations, our menu offers a diverse selection of appetizers, entrees, and desserts to suit every diner.

Warm Hospitality: We believe that the ambiance of a restaurant is as crucial as the food it serves. Our space is designed to provide a warm, inviting, and elegant setting for your dining experience. Our friendly and attentive staff is dedicated to ensuring your visit is both comfortable and memorable.

Sustainability and Community: We are committed to sustainable and ethical practices. We support local suppliers and strive to minimize our environmental impact. We believe in giving back to the community that has embraced us and continually contribute to local charities and causes.

Join Us: Come and share in our passion for exceptional food and unforgettable moments. Make a reservation or simply drop by to experience the blend of flavors and hospitality that is MDC. We look forward to hosting you and being a part of your culinary journey.

Thank you for choosing MDC as your destination for remarkable dining experiences. We can't wait to share our love for food and hospitality with you.
